A bicycle is a fun and way to move around.
It has two wheels, one behindthe other, with a seat
and handlebars to steer. You pedal with your feet to
make it go. Bicycles come in different sizes and colors.
Some havebaskets to carry things, others have bells to ring.

A bicycle, also called a pedal cycle, bike, push-bike or cycle,
is a human-powered or motor-assisted, pedal-driven, single
-track vehicle, with two wheels attached to a frame, one behind the other.
